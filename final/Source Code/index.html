Overview of Football

Football is one of America's favorite past times. From its humble beginnings to its overwhelming present popularity, football has stood the test of time and its own evolution.

Bascis of Football

Football, in its current state, is a game that consists of two teams. Each teams main goal is to take a ball down the field to score points. There are two main ways to score in the game. The first is to take the ball in the end zone, of which would score the team 6 points. The second way is to kick the ball through uprights positioned in the middle of the back of the endzone. Doing this would score 3 points for the team.

Whether on offense or defense, each team has 11 players on the field at any one time. Each position on the team has its own role. Another underlying theme for football is that in order to perform efficiently, each position needs to do its job and well for the collective to succeed.

Football has gone through a myriad of changes since its inception in the late 19th century. This website is meant to give some general knowledge about football's past, present, and potential future. Hopefully it will serve as a hub to motivate further studying about the sport.
