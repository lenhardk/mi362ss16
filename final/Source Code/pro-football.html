Professional Football

On this page you will find the current alignment of the teams within the NFL as well as the years of establishment.

NFL:
National Football League

The NFL is organized into two conferences. They are called the National Football Conference (NFC) and the American Football Conference (AFC). Both labels are developed from the legacy of the two leagues before the NFL merger in 1970. Within each conference there are 4 divisions. Each conference has a division in the North, South, East, and West. This alignment has existed since 2002, which was the last year of expansion when the Houston Texans joined the league. There have been no proposed plans for expansion in the near future, but with the NFL being as successful as it has been in the modern era, it would not be surprising to see new teams join the league and new alignments be created.
